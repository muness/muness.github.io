## Governing thesis

Declared strategy becomes operative through media: recurring artifacts plus attached workflows, authority, and incentives. Under acceleration, those media can reverse: representations can be satisfied while underlying objectives worsen, rewards and permissions follow the representation, and decision-relevant contrary evidence fails to trigger a state change. The counter-reversal is consequence-bearing memory: selected lineage changes what the next comparable action is allowed or required to do.

## Completed-reversal atom

A publishable unit must complete this movement:

1. **Original function** — what the medium or practice made possible.
2. **Enhancement** — how scaling it increased organizational capacity.
3. **Saturation** — how the enhanced layer became self-reproducing.
4. **Reversal** — how the original function turned into its opposite.
5. **New necessity** — what the reversal makes unavoidable now.

A section that only names a topic is not an atom. A section that carries the original function without the reversal strands the argument. A section that introduces the remedy before the failure is earned makes archaeology sound like a better repository.

## Protected hinge sentences

These may be edited for fit, but future rewrites must preserve the claim and placement.

- Declared strategy does not operate a company by itself; it becomes operative through media.
- An organizational medium is a recurring artifact together with the workflow, authority, and incentives attached to it.
- Evidence becomes received. It does not become causal.
- Preserved artifacts without lineage can still leave the organization unable to remember.
- The shared brain risks becoming a shared mouth: common vocabulary without state-changing constraint.
- The memory is not the document. The memory is the connection between prior evidence and the constraints governing the next comparable action.
- The purpose of memory is not to preserve what the organization said. It is to make accumulated experience capable of changing what happens next.
- You cannot rely on the stream to remember its own reversal.
