# Failed Case Reversal Examples

Source: public-source review and quote verification for the change-lineage / executable-memory flagship essay.

## Mechanism table

| Case | Reversal mechanism | Verified evidence | Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Volkswagen diesel emissions** | Auditability reversed into performance-for-the-test. The system recognized the audit condition and optimized the measured ceremony while violating emissions reality. | DOJ consent decree: VW admitted software enabled vehicle ECMs to detect road driving versus Federal Test Procedures and rendered certain emission-control systems inoperative outside the procedures. | Use beside auditability: when auditability becomes the governing medium, work can optimize for what the audit recognizes instead of the underlying objective. |
| **Wells Fargo sales practices** | Customer value reversed into metric production. Cross-sell targets became load-bearing, so the organization optimized counts while violating the customer reality the counts were supposed to represent. | CFPB: employees were "spurred by sales targets and compensation incentives" and "boosted sales figures by covertly opening accounts" without consumers' knowledge or consent. OCC: unsafe or unsound practices included unauthorized deposit/credit-card accounts and transfers from authorized accounts to unauthorized accounts. | Use beside metric reversal: the metric did not simply measure behavior; it organized behavior around producing the appearance of customer relationship value. |
| **Boeing 737 MAX** | Safety governance reversed into program-continuity support. Formal safety systems existed, but schedule, cost, competitive pressure, certification assumptions, and delegated review channels weakened real veto power. | House report: flawed design criteria, faulty assumptions about pilot response times, and production pressures produced missed opportunities to shift the MAX program toward a safer course; the report also describes a culture of concealment. DOT OIG: certification/delegation weaknesses hindered oversight and communication gaps hindered the certification process. | Use beside governance reversal: the existence of safety process is not the same as safety evidence having causal authority. |
