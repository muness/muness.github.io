Naming generic categories such as people, process, and technology does not show
why a configuration should work. State what became cheap, what disappeared or
moved, what work grew or had to be invented, who or what now supplies each
contribution, and which result would reveal a missing complement.

This keeps the framework from becoming either a defense of every existing role
or a vague instruction to combine humans and AI.
