    <header class="consultancy-page-header">
      <h1 id="contact-title">Start with the problem.</h1>
      <p>Tell me where the work breaks, who owns the decision, and what evidence would count.</p>
    </header>

    <ol class="consultancy-questions">
      <li>What work is stuck?</li>
      <li>What happens now, and where does it go wrong?</li>
      <li>Who knows the work well enough to challenge the system?</li>
      <li>Who can change the workflow or accept the risk?</li>
      <li>What would someone do differently if this worked?</li>
    </ol>

    <p class="consultancy-section__intro">Show me cases from the workflow and give someone authority to act on what we find. I do not take AI demos, tool-shopping exercises, or projects that hand the decision to the model or consultant.</p>
  </section>
