If you write first, these are the questions I care about:

- What work is stuck?
- What happens now, and where does it go wrong?
- Who knows the work well enough to challenge the system?
- Who can change the workflow or accept the risk?
- What would someone do differently if this worked?

I am usually a good fit when the problem matters, the team can show me cases from the workflow, and somebody can act on what we find. I am probably not a good fit for an AI demo, tool shopping without a workflow, or a project where you want the model or consultant to make the decision for you.
