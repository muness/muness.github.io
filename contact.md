    <header class="consultancy-page-header">
      <h1 id="contact-title">Start with one case.</h1>
      <p>You do not need a polished brief. Tell me what should have happened, who does the work today, and where the current way broke down. I can help locate whether the constraint is in the software, the rules, the evidence, the decision rights, or the way the team works.</p>
    </header>

    <ol class="consultancy-questions">
      <li>What should have happened, and what are you trying to change?</li>
      <li>Who does the work today?</li>
      <li>Where does the current way break down?</li>
      <li>What have you tried already?</li>
    </ol>

    <div class="consultancy-contact-start">
      <h2>A useful first conversation</h2>
      <p>We trace the case and identify where the likely constraint sits. Then we decide whether a deeper diagnostic or a build is worth doing. You should leave with a clearer account of the problem and the next decision; building and testing a system would be a separate step.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="consultancy-contact-fit">
      <h2>Probably not a fit</h2>
      <p>This is unlikely to work if no one who knows the job can be involved, if there is no concrete case we can examine, or if the solution has already been fixed and the job is simply to ship it.</p>
    </div>
  </section>
